Favourite Cities



Paris

Paris is one of the world's major centres of finance, commerce,
fashion and science. It is known as the 'City of Light' and is
famous for its art, fashion, and cuisine.

Good Places To Visit In PARIS :

    • The Eiffel Tower
    • Louvre Museum
    • Notre Dame Cathedral


Chennai

Chennai is the capital of Tamil Nadu, which is located on the
Coromandel coast of the Bay of Bengal in south India. Madras
was the name given to Chennai in the past.

Good Places To Visit In CHENNAI :

    • Kapaleeshwarar Temple
    • San Thome Church
    • Marina Beach


New York

New York is the most ethnically diverse, religiously varied,
commercially driven, famously congested, and in the eyes
of many as the most attractive urban centre in the country.

Good Places To Visit In NEW YORK :

    • Statue Of Liberty
    • Central Park
    • Empire State Building
